AyurAid Data Deletion Policy

AyurAid is committed to protecting your privacy. If you wish to have your personal data deleted from our records, please follow the process outlined below:

Data Deletion Requests

To request the deletion of your data, please send an email to suyashvashishtha@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." In the email, include the following details:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your AyurAid account
  • A clear statement that you wish to have your data deleted

We will process your request promptly, and you will receive confirmation once your data has been deleted. Please note that certain data may be retained for a specific period as required by applicable laws or for legitimate business purposes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Data Deletion Policy, please contact us at suyashvashishtha@gmail.com.
